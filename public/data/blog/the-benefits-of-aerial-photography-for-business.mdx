## **Improves Safety and Security**

Aerial photography can play an important role in property safety and security. By providing a bird's eye view of the surroundings, it can help to identify potential hazards and develop emergency response plans.

For businesses, this can include monitoring the condition of roofs, identifying areas prone to flooding or wildfires, and assessing the security of premises.

## **Better Planning and Decision Making**

Aerial photography is an extremely useful tool for businesses when it comes to [property planning and management](https://www.42interactive.com/services/test-property-management-technology). It gives businesses a birds-eye view of their property, and allows them to make more informed decisions about how to best utilize their resources.

For example, a business can use aerial photography to optimize their layout, or to determine which areas need improvement. Additionally, aerial photography can help businesses understand the environmental impact of their decisions, and ensure they are compliant with any relevant regulations.

## **Environmental Monitoring**

Aerial photography provides an overview of an area that can be used for [environmental monitoring](https://www.heavy.ai/technical-glossary/environmental-monitoring). This includes tracking changes in vegetation, water quality, and land use. businesses that need to comply with environmental regulations can benefit from using aerial photography to monitor the health of forests, assess the impact of mining or drilling activities, and track the spread of invasive species.
