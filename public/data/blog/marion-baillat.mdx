We are extremely pleased to announce that we have a new member to the [**42Interactive**](/) family for 2019.  An amazing 2019 awaits [**42 Interactive**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/42-interactive/) with the addition of our Executive Producer, Marion Baillat, to the fold. With years of hands on involvement in producing creative and strategically driven experiences, utilising various technology; she has helped deliver creative digital experiences for companies such as such as Samsung,  EAGames, Tetra Pak, Havaianas, Audi, Lendlease, Stockland, Sydney Opera House, Telstra and others.

![photo of marion baillat](https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/611c5611c7286822ba86a62c/6130be635cc167dfd38ae87a_profile-marion-2019-768x1024.jpeg)

With a passion for wide range of exciting and upcoming areas such as Photonics, Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), Mixed Reality (MR), Robotics, Holography coupled with her love of Japanese Anime, Visual Art and Psychology (and being able to speak 4 languages) our team and our partners look forward to learning more from Marion in the future.From Software applications development, CMS, Digital Signage, Experiential Digital Activations, Marion has managed a spectrum of large projects and budgets and her innovation and commercialization experience has consistently delivered results.As the Executive producer for **42 Interactive** she will be ensuring the alignment of our design and development teams both in [**Australia (Sydney)**](/) and [**Indonesia (Bali)**](/) overseas and ensuring our Partners’ deliverables continue to be met, but she will be also provide positive support to other business units such as [**Propviz**](https://propviz.com.au/) and [**NeuroTechnology.**](https://neurotechnology.com.au/)
