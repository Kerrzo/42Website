**Getting locked down at home during Covid-19 quarantine** does not mean you can't have a good time. Here are **cool - fun (AND FREE!) apps** you can download and play at home.

Pass the time with a couple of **FREE mobile games** that you might find fun if you like [FOOD](https://gargleblastergames.com/food-escape/) or are into sports, especially [CRICKET](https://gargleblastergames.com/sandyballscricket-2/).

If you are looking for some **calm and relaxation** and have **VR Headset** such as **HTC Vive** or **Google Cardboard** or similar then we recommend [Instacalm VR](https://neurotechnology.com.au/instacalm-vr-relaxation-therapy/) to have a chill. Available on [STEAM VR](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1153860/Instacalm_VR/),  [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/au/app/instacalm-vr/id1482864843) and [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/dev?id=7613087702652735885&hl=en_AU).

The **iOS and Android versions** of Instacalm VR are currently **FREE** for a limited time and the **Steam VR version** heavily discounted.

If you are into **making games** then there is also a **FREE demo plugin** in the [Unity Asset Store](https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/vfx/shaders/x-ray-plug-in-155735) that can help you improve Unity [project](/digital-agency-projects-accolades) and allow you to **look through walls**?

Enable your software with the paid [full version XRay plugin](https://gargleblastergames.com/xray/) or the [free version from the Unity Asset Store.](https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/vfx/shaders/x-ray-plug-in-155735) Full [Tutorial Here](https://gargleblastergames.com/x_ray-plugin-tutorial/).  

Stay safe and enjoy.
