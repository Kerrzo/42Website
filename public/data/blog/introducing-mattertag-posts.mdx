## What is Mattertag?

Mattertag is a clever way to add context to specific locations, objects, and features inside your virtual video content. It is achieved by adding a title and text description anchored right to a point in a space of the video.

Apply the tagging to highlight key features and provide a more detailed explanation. This nice little touch goes a long way in optimizing your virtual content. It will add leverage to your Matterport 3D videos as a more effective marketing, workflow, and storytelling tool.
