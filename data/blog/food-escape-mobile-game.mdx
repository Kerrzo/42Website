**Late last year one of 42Interactive projects,** [**Gargle Blaster Games**](https://gargleblastergames.com/) **launched its newest mobile game - FOOD ESCAPE.**

Food escape mobile game is a simple swipe left and right to avoid the obstacles and harvest gold coins. This [game](/digital-transformation-agency) is suitable for all ages. Here is the idea of this game.

In a secret science lab, a mad scientist is experimenting on food to see if they can be weaponized.

With a freak science coincidence, the food came to life.

When they know that they’re trapped and experimented on, the escape plan is on.

## Can You Help the Food to Escape?

How far can you go?

It’s not going to be that easy to help the food escape!
