### **Audience-Focused Content**

‍

This is the most important, but yet the most common mistake content creator makes when it comes to creating content for their business. It's crucial that you understand the point and goal of this strategy, as it plays important role for your business' overall performance and result.

‍

No matter how much content you create, how many related hashtags you throw, and how creative it looks, as long as you're still posting the same 'egocentric content' on your socials, you will only waste your time and efforts.

‍

Although it's not really a secret anymore, but the most effective way to gain more attraction and engagement to your social media is to create content that your ideal audience need, for Ex. what kind of problem they want to solve, what do they struggling with, what kind of topic they want to explore, what kind of various of answers they want to find and what tips they would love to hear, so it's important to keep your audience in mind when it comes to brainstorming and creating your social content.

‍

The most effective way to gather information about your target audience is to create your audience persona or avatar. A target audience or customer persona is a semi-fictional representation of your ideal audience or customer based on actual data from a detailed market research, it usually contains personal information (age, gender, hobbies, location ,income etc), pain point, goals, challenges, value and fears.

‍

By creating a target audience persona you will understand your audience better, and it will help you make a more effective content strategy that relatable to your target audience.

## Learn From Competitors

Learning from your competitors is one of the most effective ways to come up with new and innovative ideas for your content. However, it's important to not simply copy what they're doing, but to be inspired by their successes and failures in order to expand upon their ideas.
