**_Curious about how Augmented Reality \[AR\] can transform customer experience?_**

‍

In an increasingly digitised world, **innovative technologies** are reshaping how businesses connect with customers. **Augmented Reality (AR)** stands out as a game-changing tech, blending digital elements seamlessly with reality.

Originally for gaming, AR now [transforms](https://www.42interactive.com/) industries, enhancing customer experiences. This piece explores AR's exciting realm, revealing how businesses can elevate interactions, leading to **deeper engagement and satisfaction**.

From personalised products to immersive visualisation, discover how Augmented Reality is **revolutionising customer experiences**.

‍

**The Client**

‍

Lathams, a **family-owned business** established in the early 1950s, originally dealt with materials handling, power transmission equipment, roller-chains, sprockets, and custom machinery. Over time, the company shifted its focus towards the **terrazzo, marble, granite, and stone industries**.

They introduced innovations like the **Neoprene-X-Pansion Loc Strip Control Joint System**, producing various products such as dividing strips, flexible expansion joint covers, safety stair tread nosings, and more.

**The Challenges**

‍

-   **Technical Integration Complexity:** Integrating AR into existing Content Management System \[CMS\] requires compatibility with various devices, browsers, and platforms, which lead to technical challenges that might slow down implementation.
-   **Content Creation and Management:** Creating and managing AR content, such as 3D models and interactive elements, demands new skills and workflows. It means ensuring a streamlined process for content creation and updates is crucial.
-   **User Experience:** If not seamlessly integrated, AR features might disrupt the website's user experience, causing confusion or frustration among visitors. Thus, ensuring a smooth and intuitive interface is vital.

‍

**The Solutions**

‍

Faced with the hurdles of integrating an AR [solution](https://www.42interactive.com/services/immersive-xr-development) into Latham Australia's website CMS, 42 Interactive team devised the following solutions:

**The Benefits**

‍

Below are several potential benefits of incorporating AR into Latham Australia's website CMS:

‍

-   **Enhanced User Engagement:** The AR content provides an interactive and immersive experience for users, capturing their attention and encouraging them to spend more time on their website.
-   **Brand Differentiation:** Implementing AR showcases Latham Australia's brand commitment to innovation and customer experience, setting it apart from competitors.
-   **Boosted SEO and Traffic:** AR implementations can attract attention and inbound links, contributing to improved search engine rankings and increased website traffic.
-   **Data Insights:** AR interactions provide valuable data about user behaviour, preferences, and engagement, which can be used to refine marketing strategies and product offerings.
-   **Cross-Platform Accessibility:** Custom AR solutions can be accessed across various devices, including smartphones, tablets, and wearable devices, ensuring a wider reach.
-   **Improved Product Understanding:** AR enables users to view products in 3D, enhancing their understanding of complex products or services.
