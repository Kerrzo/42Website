Have you ever experienced a pain that stays for so long, and is hard to manage? You tried pain killers but they only reduced it temporarily. What if there's a way to manage your chronic pain without having to take any medication?

‍

Chronic pain affects thousands of people all around the world, and there aren't many effective solution options to effectively help manage the pain, many of whom have tried numerous surgical and chemical options unsuccessfully.

‍

[Neurotechnology](https://www.linkedin.com/company/neurotechnologyaustralia/) : Innovate Pain Clinic aims to help to solve these cases, we help pain sufferers to manage their pain effectively with a method which not involves drugs and surgery. We introduce individualised therapeutic VR experiences to induce pain relief using a cutting-edge technology called Tranceducer™ and tailor the VR experience for maximum therapeutic advantage using CognitiVR™.

‍

We had an amazing opportunity to share our thoughts and innovative solutions on [LiSTNR](https://www.linkedin.com/company/listnrau/) : 'Beyond The Medicine Cabinet' hosted by Dr's On-Call Manager, Zoe Callister-Hakewill. Listen to this podcast to find out how can you manage your chronic pain with the help of virtual reality technology!
