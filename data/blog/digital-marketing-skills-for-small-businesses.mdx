Digital marketing is now a crucial part of any companies work nowadays in order to stay competitive with others, you need the [digital](https://www.42interactive.com/) talents and skills. Failure in strategy implementation might arise if you don’t use the right strategy or right digital marketing skills.

Therefore, here are some of the most impactful digital marketing skills you should have for small businesses to execute good digital marketing:

## Communication

Digital marketing will involve diverse teams and audiences. Thus an excellent communication skill in digital marketing is crucial.  An excellent communication skill is needed to create impactful messages, see things from a different perspectives and deliver the ideas or messages in a way that audiences will understand.

## Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

With the process of improving a website and online presence, Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) is a key to digital marketing. Regardless of how great your content is without SEO, the content won't get visibility which reduces its ROI. Thus, an understanding of best practises and content optimisation are essential to run successful digital marketing campaign.

Having a solid knowledge and understanding in SEO or even better having a SEO specialist is a great way to run a successful digital marketing campaign.

## Email Marketing

Email marketing is a way to actively engage people of already interested in your [businesses](https://www.42interactive.com/about-us), products and services. Email campaigns are therefore also an important skill to have in digital marketing. You will need to understand the best practises in email and copy to optimise your marketing strategies.

Your marketing team will need to have the ability to write different types of emails such as newsletters, products or services update, company news, etc.

## Paid Advertising

Paid advertising is a type of advertising that you have to pay for. You will have to pay for an ad space and the price paid for the ad space is often settled in a bidding between the users (businesses who use the space) and the ad space owner.

Paid advertising is important in digital marketing as it is an effective way to expose your businesses’ brand/name to your target audience and to increase traffic. This is also good to further customise your business marketing campaigns.

There are several types of paid advertisings such as [Pay-Per-Click (PPC),](https://g.co/kgs/fwPW8Q) [Google Ads](https://g.co/kgs/3qBmiu), [Facebook Ads](https://web.facebook.com/business/ads?_rdc=1&_rdr), [Linkedin Ads](https://bit.ly/3SoBvvw), [Twitter Ads](https://ads.twitter.com/onboarding/18ce55iylis/welcome), etc.

## Social Media

In digital marketing, knowledge and skill on how to use social media channels is one of the keys to connect to your target audience by setting up engaging campaigns and efficiently creating and scheduling posts that will increase engagement.

Each social media channel has its own parameter and idiosyncrasies to help businesses target their contents to audience. Thus, the skills to create high-quality contents, ensuring the quality of the contents, the relevancy of the contents and engagement with the target audience are also important.

## Blogging

Most of modern businesses especially digital businesses, blogging is part of their digital marketing campaigns.

There are benefits of blogging such as:

-   Helps your businesses’ awareness online, which will support its growth
-   Helps your website traffic
-   Helps converting the website traffic into leads. Moreover, each new post will have an opportunity to generate new leads.
-   Helps your social media channels’ presence going. \[If your social media team is stuck with ideas of new contents, they can use the blogs as their repository of content\].
-   Share your company news. \[You can highlight what your company is up to, such as celebrations, anniversaries, team members, etc. Not only sharing your company news sound human, but it also helps your audience see that your company isn’t always selling\]

## Other Skills

Apart from the above important skills, these skills will also be great value to have in a digital marketing team:

‍

### Data Analysis

A procedure to gather various information from multiple online sources of your target market. It is recommended for your Digital Marketing team to have these skills, that will help them to analyse and make effective marketing plans or strategies for your company’s marketing campaigns.

### Basic Design Skills

It is also good for your Digital Marketing team to have basic design skills and have knowledge on design softwares such as Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Canva, etc. It will help them to create visual contents that will get more exposure than written contents.

### Customer Relation Management (CRM)

Having the basic skill in customer relation management is valuable in digital marketing and for your Digital Marketing team – to understand your customers and ensure that they are satisfied.

### Basic Web design

Having knowledge on a web design will be valuable for your digital marketing team such as how to incorporate the company logo, creating a blog draft in website, creating a web form, etc.  

### Tech Savvy

Getting familiar with commonly used softwares and tools is can be beneficial for your digital marketing team, especially in digital companies.

### Creative Thinking and Persuasive

Having a combination skill in [creative](https://www.42interactive.com/services/creative-technology-consultancy) thinking and be persuasive will also be beneficial for your digital marketing team and your digital company, as it help them to come up with a various creative and innovative marketing campaign ideas to drive your business forward.
