WHAT WE'VE DONE

We designed and developed an VR Psychotherapy Experience using Neuroplasticity to help people manage their chronic pain.

Pioneering a VR therapy experience in a world-first innovative clinic in Edgecliff, NSW, Australia.

90% of patients managed to significantly reduce their chronic pain after just three visits.

Curing chronic pain through a blend of VR technology and neuroplasticity.

INNOVATION IN MEDICAL TECHNOLOGY

The program includes 12 training modules designed to help patients understand and manage pain. Using CognitiVR, Innovate Pain Clinic introduces a new approach to pain treatment based on neuroplasticity, guiding patients through immersive VR experiences that promote pain relief. This project demonstrates how creative technology can deliver real, practical impact in healthcare — solving real problems, not serving as a marketing gimmick.
